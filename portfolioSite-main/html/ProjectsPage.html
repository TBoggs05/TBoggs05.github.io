PROJECTS

LIGHTSTOP

MOBILE GAME

Available for download on IOS and android

Lightstop is a mobile game built using Unity and C# over the course of a couple weeks.

Cool things implemented:

-Unity Ads API
-polar coordinates for calculating lines at runtime
-iterative mergesort for reorganizing cells.


TAILS OF TWO

2-DAY GAME JAM

Available to play on Itch

Tails of Two is my submission to the "Jame-Gam Mini" Game Jam.


I led a team of 3 inexperienced friends to make this in 2 days.

While the game is not particularly impressive, I believe it demonstrates my ability to work in a time crunch, adapt to situational constraints, and lead/teach team members.
